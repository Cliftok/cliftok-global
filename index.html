About Us

Cliftok Global Resources is a Nigerian company specializing in the supply of skilled and semi-skilled labour to the oil and gas industry. We currently supply manpower to PHRC and are expanding into general oilfield support services and supplies. We are committed to safety, reliability, and high-quality service delivery.
